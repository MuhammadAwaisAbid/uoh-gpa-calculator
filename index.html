University of Haripur Grading System

Official Grading System (Effective 2023)

This table shows the conversion of marks to grade points according to the University of Haripur regulations.

Percentage Letter Grade Grade Point
85% and above A 4.00
80% - 84% A- 3.50 - 3.99
75% - 79% B+ 3.00 - 3.49
70% - 74% B 2.50 - 2.99
65% - 69% B- 2.00 - 2.49
60% - 64% C+ 1.50 - 1.99
55% - 59% C 1.00 - 1.49
50% - 54% D 0.50 - 0.99
Below 50% F 0.00

Important Notes

  • CGPA is calculated as the weighted average of GPA of all semesters
  • Each semester's GPA is weighted by its total credit hours
  • Minimum CGPA for degree completion: 2.00
